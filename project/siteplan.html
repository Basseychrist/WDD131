Site Plan

Site Name

Selected Site Name: Urban Gardening Network

Reason: This name represents a community-focused platform dedicated to urban gardening enthusiasts, providing resources, tips, and a forum for exchanging ideas and experiences.

Optional Domain Availability: urbangardeningnetwork.org

Site Purpose

The site aims to create a central hub for urban gardening enthusiasts, offering information on best practices, gardening techniques, and resources for starting and maintaining an urban garden. It will also include a community forum for members to share their experiences and ask for advice.

Scenarios

Scenario 1: How can I start an urban garden in a small apartment space?

Scenario 2: Where can I find local community gardens to join?
